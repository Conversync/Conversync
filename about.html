Our Story

The journey from frustrated customers to AI pioneers transforming customer service.

2019

The Problem Becomes Clear

After countless experiences with poor customer service automation, our founders realized that businesses were forced to choose between cost-effective bots and quality human interaction. There had to be a better way.

2020

Research & Development

We began deep research into conversational AI, studying thousands of customer interactions to understand what makes conversations feel natural, helpful, and genuinely human.

2021

First Breakthrough

Our prototype AI successfully handled complex restaurant reservations with interruption handling and natural conversation flow. The first business to try it saw a 300% increase in bookings.

2022

Company Founded

Conversync officially launched with seed funding from leading AI investors. Our mission: make every customer interaction exceptional through human-like AI technology.

2023

Rapid Growth

Reached 1,000+ businesses using our AI receptionist. Expanded from restaurants to healthcare, legal services, and retail with consistently outstanding results.

2024

Advanced AI Platform

Launched our comprehensive AI platform with both call agents and chatbots. Processed over 1 million customer conversations with industry-leading satisfaction scores.

2025

The Future of Customer Service

Today, we're proud to serve 15,000+ businesses worldwide, handling millions of conversations with AI that customers often prefer over human agents. This is just the beginning.
