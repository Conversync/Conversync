Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our AI solutions pricing

AI Call Agent uses a monthly subscription with credit points based on usage, perfect for phone-based customer service. AI Chatbot is a one-time purchase with unlimited usage, ideal for website and messaging support.

Credits are consumed based on call duration and complexity. Simple FAQ calls use 20-30 credits, appointment bookings use 40-60 credits, and complex consultations use 80-120 credits. Unused credits roll over monthly.

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for the AI Call Agent with full access to all features. No credit card required. For the AI Chatbot, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

No setup fees, no hidden costs. Call Agent pricing includes everything you need. Chatbot is a one-time purchase with lifetime updates included. Additional services like custom integrations are optional.

For Call Agent plans, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. For Chatbot, you can upgrade from Basic to Advanced by paying the difference.

You'll receive notifications when approaching your limit. You can purchase additional credits or upgrade your plan. We never cut off service unexpectedly - you'll always have buffer time to adjust your plan.
