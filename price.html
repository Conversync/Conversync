How Credit Points Are Used

Each AI call your business receives consumes credit points from your plan. The number of points depends on the complexity and length of the call.

20–30

Basic Call

Short calls, like answering simple FAQs or providing information. Points are deducted automatically per call.

40–60

Booking Call

Calls that schedule appointments or require multiple steps. Points are deducted automatically per call.

80–120

Complex Call

Multi-step consultations or detailed guidance. Points are deducted automatically per call.

Unused credits roll over to the next month. You can purchase additional credits or upgrade your plan if needed. Your dashboard always shows your current point balance so you know exactly how much you have left.
